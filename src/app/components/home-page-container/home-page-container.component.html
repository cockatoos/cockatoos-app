The Problem Statement
Like many non-native English speakers, I struggle with certain English pronunciation. There is no existing platform that allows me to practice my speaking by providing feedback on how understandable I sound and pointing out the nuances in my pronunciation. Furthermore, hiring a speech coach for speech training can easily cost up to 200 pounds per hour.
Our Solution
Cockatoos is an intuitive speech training platform that gives instant feedback on a user's vocal delivery. The two aspects we chose to focus on:
  • Pronunciation - the correctness in how letters sound
  • Articulation - the clarity of sounds produced
With these tools, a user can build confidence in speaking and improve their ability to be understood.
