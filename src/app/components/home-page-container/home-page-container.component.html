Our Mission
Democratise the Learning of Speaking
The idea of Cockatoos originates from my own struggle in softening my English accent. Hiring a speech coach could easily cost 200 pounds per hour, which is too expensive for most people. We hope our app can provide a platform that allows people to:
  • Practice speaking a new language using the Echo Method
  • Get instant feedback on how their accent sounds like from our Machine Learning model
  • Improve their speaking skills without spending a lot of money

Our target audiences are people such as language learners and actors who want to soften their accent or pick up a new accent in a certain language.
